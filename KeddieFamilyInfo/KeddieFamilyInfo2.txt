                       Some thing About Howard's Forebears

        Rather than try to work out a family tree, 1'm going to make
an effort to give somewhat of a run-down of what I know about some
of my ancestors.
        We'll start with the Keddie side: My great grandfather was
George Keddie, born May 22, 1798, Fife Co. Scotland and died 1857
at Falkirk, Scotland.
        My great grandmother was Agnes Walkenshaw, born Oct. 21, 1816
in Scotland. She died 1887 in Falkirk, Scotland.
        My other great grandfather and grandmother, parents of my
grandmother were Henry Taylor and Agnes Leech. The dates of their
births is unknown, but in Scotland. Agnes died in 1903.

                    ------------------

        My grandfather was Thomas Keddie, born June 21, 1844 near
Falkirk, Scotland, and died Feb. 17, 1927, Sylvia, Kans.
        My grandmother was Janet Taylor Keddie, born Aug. 9, 1844,
in Scotland, and died Feb. 11, 1922, Sylvia. Both are buried in
the community cemetery near Sylvia.  Fairview cemetery

                    ------------------

Note: The grandparents, on his mother's side, of Thomas Keddie
were Frank Walkenshaw and Agnes Keer.

                     -----------------

        Thomas Keddie came to the United States in the late 1870s
and first settled in Pittsburg. He was a cabinet maker, and it is
said that he built the steps in the old Pittsburg City Hall.
Just when he and my grandmother were married is not known.
Sometime in the 1877 they moved to Kansas, near Sylvia, where my
grandfather took up a timber claim, which was farm land that to
government gave in return for planting so many acres of trees. He
also purchased what was known as railroad land. Land given to the
railroads to be sold to get money to build railroads, in this case
the Atchison, Topeka and the Santa Fe.

        To Thomas and Janet were born Agnes, Jan. 18,1869, in Scotland;
George, Mar. 9, 1871 at Pittsburgh; Jessie, Sept. 15, 1877, Sylvia;
Annie, Oct. 26, 1880, Sylvia; Henry, Mar.9, 1882, Sylvia; and
Thomas (my father) Apr. 16, 1884, Sylvia.

        The following is who each of those married, and their children:
Agnes - John Withroder: Everett, Thomas, Edward, George. Eleta, Gladys.
George- Mary Hamilton: George and Wilbur.
Jessie - George Ankerholz: Edith, Guy and Ada.
Annie - Howard Dennis: Henry.
Henry - Janie Davis; Winona, Eldred and Warren
Thomas - Luttie Rhiter; Howard and Dorothy.

                     |||||
                        --------------

        Grandfather Keddie had the following brothers and sisters:
Agnes (Sharp), George, Annie (Nichelson), Thomas (Our granfather),
Jessie (Todd), Frank, Adam, George and Mary (Todd). Little is know
what became of them. I believe George came to the U.S. It is thought
that Adam died at sea and was buried there.

        Grandmother Keddie's brothers and sisters: Alexander Taylor,
Janet (Keddie), George, William, Marion, Jean (Spence), Lizzie (Wilson).
To my knowledge only one left Scotland and came to Canada.
