                    Keddie Family Information

        To put some light on my forebears, we'll go back as far as we
have any information. Two of my great, great grandparents were
Frank and Agnes (Keer) Walkenshaw. We believe they lived in Fife
County, Scotland. Grandparents of Thomas Keddie, my grandfather

        My great grandfather was George Keddie, Born May 22,1798, Fife
County, Scotland and died 1857 at Falkirk, Scotland. He married
Agnes Walkenshaw, born Oct. 21, 1816 in Scotland, and died 1887
in Falkirk.

        My other great grandfather and great grandmother, parents of
my grandmother, Janet Keddie, were Henry and Agnes (Leech) Taylor.
The dates of their births are unknown. Agnes died in 1903.

                        ----------------

        My grandfather was Thomas Keddie, born June 21, 1844, near
Falkirk, Scotland, and died Feb. 17, 1927, Sylvia Kansas.

        My grandmother was Janet (Taylor) Keddie, born Aug. 9, 1844,
in Scotland, and died Feb. 11, 1922, Sylvia. Both are buried in
the Fairview Cemetery, north of Sylvia.

                        ----------------

        Thomas Keddie married Janet Taylor in 1868, and they came to
the United States in 1870, and first settled in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania.
He was a cabinet maker, and it is said that he built the original
steps in the Pittsburgh City Hall.

        In about 1877 they moved to Kansas, near Sylvia, where they
settled on a timber claim, which was farm land given by the govern-
ment in return for planting so many acres of trees. He also pur-
chased railroad land. Land given to the railroads to be sold to
raise money to build railroads, in this case the Atchison, Topeka
and the Santa Fe.

        Children born to Thomas and Janet Keddie were: Agnes, Jan. 18,
1869, in Scotland; George, Mar.9, 1871 at Pittsburgh; Jessie, Sept.
15, 1877, Sylvia" Annie, Oct. 26,1880, sylvia; Henry, Mar.9, 1882
and Thomas (my father) Apr. 16, 1884, Sylvia.

        This is who each of those married and their children:
Agnes - John Withroder: Everett, Thomas, Edward, George, Eleta, Gladys.
George - Mary Hamilton: George and Wilbur.
Jessie - George Ankerholz: Edith, Guy and Ada.
Annie - Howard Dennis: Henry.
Henry - Janie Davis: Winona, Eldred and Warren.
Thomas - Luttie Rhiter: Howard and Dorothy

                        ----------------

        Grandfather Keddie had these brothers and sisters: Agnes (Sharp),
George, Annie (Nichelson), Thomas (our grandfather), Jessie (Todd),
Frank, Adam, George and Mary (Todd). Little is known what became of
them. I believe George came to the U.S, It is thought Adam died at
sea and was buried there.

        Grandmother Keddie's bothers and sisters: Alexander Taylor,
Janet (Keddie), George, William, Marion, Jean (Spence), Lizzie (Wilson).
One of the girls came to Canada.
